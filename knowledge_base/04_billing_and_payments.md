# Billing & Payments Guide

## Billing Cycle
- All plans are billed monthly on the first of each month
- Annual plans are billed upfront with a 15% discount
- Invoices are sent to the billing email on file 3 days before the charge date

## How to View Your Invoice
1. Log into the AutoVoice Dashboard
2. Navigate to: Account → Billing → Invoice History
3. Download any invoice as a PDF

## Understanding Your Invoice
Each invoice includes:
- **Base plan charge:** Your monthly plan fee (Starter/Growth/Enterprise)
- **Voice overage:** Minutes used beyond your plan limit × $0.08/min
- **SMS overage:** SMS credits used beyond your plan limit × $0.02/SMS
- **Add-on charges:** Express onboarding, additional phone numbers, etc.
- **Taxes:** Sales tax based on your dealership's state

## Updating Payment Method
1. Go to: Account → Billing → Payment Methods
2. Click "Add New Card"
3. Enter card details (Visa, Mastercard, Amex accepted)
4. Set as default
5. Your old card will be charged one final time if there are any outstanding balances

We do NOT accept ACH/bank transfers or checks for monthly billing. Enterprise clients may request invoice-based NET-30 billing.

## Failed Payments
If a payment fails:
- **Day 1:** Automatic retry
- **Day 3:** Second retry + email notification to billing contact
- **Day 7:** Third retry + warning email
- **Day 10:** Account suspended (read-only mode — calls stop being answered)
- **Day 30:** Account terminated and data scheduled for deletion

To reactivate a suspended account, update your payment method and contact support for manual reactivation.

## Plan Upgrades and Downgrades
- **Upgrading:** Effective immediately. You are charged a prorated amount for the remainder of the current billing cycle
- **Downgrading:** Effective at the start of the next billing cycle. You retain your current plan features until then
- Downgrades that would cause overage usage to exceed the new plan limits will be flagged before confirmation

## Cancellation Policy
- Cancel anytime from: Account → Billing → Cancel Subscription
- Service continues until the end of the current paid billing period
- No refunds for partial months
- 30-day data retention after cancellation — export your data before this window closes
- Re-subscribing within 30 days restores your data and settings

## Refund Policy
AutoVoice AI offers refunds in the following situations:
- Service outage exceeding 4 hours in a billing month: credit equivalent to 1 day of service
- Billing errors (double charges, wrong plan charged): full refund processed within 5 business days
- First-month dissatisfaction within 14 days of signup: full refund (one-time, new customers only)

To request a refund, contact support with your invoice number and reason.

## Common Billing Questions

**Q: Why did my bill increase this month?**
A: Most increases are due to voice/SMS overages. Check your usage in Dashboard → Analytics → Usage Report. If you're regularly hitting overages, upgrading your plan is more cost-effective.

**Q: I was charged twice this month**
A: This can happen if a payment failed and was retried. Check your invoice history. If two separate invoices show the same billing period, contact support — this is a billing error and will be refunded.

**Q: Can I get a discount for multiple dealership locations?**
A: Yes. Enterprise plans support multiple locations under one account with volume discounts. Contact our sales team at sales@autovoiceai.com for a custom quote.
