# AutoVoice AI — Product Overview

## What is AutoVoice AI?
AutoVoice AI is a B2B SaaS platform that provides AI-powered voice and SMS agents for car dealerships across the United States. Our AI agents handle inbound and outbound calls, qualify leads, schedule service appointments, answer inventory questions, and manage after-hours communications — all without requiring a human operator.

## Core Products

### 1. VoiceBot (Inbound Call Handler)
- Answers all inbound calls 24/7
- Handles: appointment scheduling, service reminders, inventory FAQs, financing inquiries
- Escalates to a live agent when confidence is low or customer requests it
- Supports custom dealership greetings and scripts

### 2. TextBot (SMS Agent)
- Responds to inbound SMS leads from sources like Cars.com, CarGurus, AutoTrader
- Sends appointment confirmations and reminders
- Handles opt-out/opt-in per TCPA regulations automatically

### 3. Outbound Dialer (ProActive)
- Calls lapsed customers for service reminders
- Follows up on unsold leads from the CRM
- All outbound calls include consent verification
